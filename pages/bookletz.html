BOOKLETz

   BOOKLETz is a room booking website that implements CRUD functionality. The platform includes a login and registration system, with distinct roles for admins and users. Users can register and log in to book rooms, specifying the date and time for their booking. The system automatically associates the booking with the username of the logged-in user. Admin accounts, however, are created directly in the database and cannot be registered through the user interface.
